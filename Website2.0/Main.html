NetworkOfProductivity

Welcome to NetworkOfProductivity!

The goal of the website is to make education easier for you! At the moment, we want to be able to provide students resources to better their learning experience. With this website, our goal is to make learning as simple as possible! We would provide resources for things like university courses such as mathematics, computer science, economics, psychology and much more. We also want to add resources to help other students save money by providing textbook pdf's on the internet and other resources used within their courses.

Besides the resource aspect of school, we want to provide users with the ability to check out playlists that we like, chat with other students from either their university or other universities, and connect with one another. This website designed for students and people alike, therefore, it is very community based. We love to take feedback from you as we continue to improve our website. Send an email to us to add new features and we will try to get back to you as soon as possible! Hope you enjoy and have fun! - Hayden
